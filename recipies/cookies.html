Cookies

Warm, melt in your mouth cookies that friends and family will be in love with!

Back in the day, I used to go out to the markets to fetch the warmest fresh baked cookies that I could find. Turns out, these cookies can be created right at home. Follow these simple steps and soon you will be screaming aloud, "I am cookie monster .... feed me cookies", or something like that, I haven't seen sesame street in quite some time. Okay cheers!

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cups brown sugar
  • 2 sticks salted butter
  • 2 eggs (room temperature)
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 bag semisweet chocolate chips (12 oz)

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F (175 C)
  2. Combine sugars and butter. Use electric mixer to beat together until pale yellow and texture is creamy. Slowly beat in eggs and vanilla
  3. In a seperate bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt
  4. Stir in flour mixture a little bit at a time until fully combined. Add chocolate chips
  5. Form into 1-inch patties on parchment paper and bake for 15-17 mins or until golden brown with crispy edges
