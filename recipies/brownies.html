Brownies

The most tasty brownies in your entire life

Get ready to wow guests with this insanely tasty treat, the brownie. Origin date unkown, though many have said the ancient romans were obsessed with this simple yet delicious treat

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup salted butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven 350 F (175 C)
  2. In large saucepan, melt 1/2 cup butter
  3. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla
  4. Beat in 1/3 cup cocoa, 1/2 cup flour, salt, and baking powder
  5. Spread batter into prepared pan. Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes
