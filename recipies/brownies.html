Brownies

The most tasty brownies in your entire life

Get ready to wow guests with this insanely tasty treat, the brownie. Origin date unkown, though many have said the ancient romans were obsessed with this simple yet delicious treat

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup salted butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
